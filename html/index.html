    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I schedule a service appointment?

    You can schedule a service appointment in several ways: through our online booking system, by calling our service center at (123) 456-7890, or by visiting our location in person. Our online booking system is available 24/7 and allows you to select your preferred date and time.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, cash, and personal checks. We also offer financing options for larger repairs through our financing partners.

    Do you offer a warranty on your services?

    Yes, we stand behind our work. All of our repairs come with a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on parts and labor. Some services may have different warranty terms, which will be clearly explained before any work is performed.

    How long does a typical service take?

    Service times vary depending on the type of service needed. Basic services like oil changes typically take 30-60 minutes, while more complex repairs may take several hours or even multiple days. When you schedule your appointment, we'll provide an estimated completion time.

    Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

    Yes, we offer pickup and delivery services within a 10-mile radius of our location for an additional fee. We also provide a complimentary shuttle service for customers who need to get to work or home while their vehicle is being serviced.
